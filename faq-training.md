Tinitiate AI Training Solutions — Training FAQ
## What type of training does Tinitiate AI provide?

Tinitiate AI provides IT training programs for both beginners and professionals. The training focuses on practical learning, hands-on projects, interview preparation, resume support, mock interviews, and placement assistance.

## What are the training categories available?

Tinitiate AI offers two main training categories:

Beginner Training — for students, freshers, and learners starting from basics.
Professional Training — for learners who want job-focused, advanced, industry-level skills.

## Which courses are available under Beginner Training?

Beginner Training includes courses such as Java Basics, Python, JavaScript / HTML / CSS, TypeScript, SQL, and Data Structures.

## Which courses are available under Professional Training?

Professional Training includes Java Full Stack Training, React Web Development, Next.js Web Development, Full Stack Angular + .NET, AWS Data Engineering, Snowflake Data Engineering, Azure Data Engineering, GCP Data Engineering, and Visualization.

## Is the training online or offline?

The listed training programs are offered in Remote / Online mode, so students can attend the sessions from anywhere.

## Who conducts the training?

The training is conducted by industry-experienced professionals, helping students learn with real-time and career-focused guidance.

Yes. Tinitiate AI training includes live interactive sessions, so students can learn directly from trainers and clarify doubts during the sessions.

Yes. The training includes hands-on projects and real-time project experience, helping learners understand practical implementation, not just theory.

## Is interview preparation included?

Yes. Both Beginner and Professional Training programs include interview preparation and mock interviews to help students prepare for job opportunities.

## Does Tinitiate AI provide resume building support?

## Is placement assistance provided?

Yes. Tinitiate AI provides placement assistance along with training, resume support, and mock interview preparation.
