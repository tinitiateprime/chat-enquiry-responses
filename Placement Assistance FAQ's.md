Yes. Tinitiate AI provides placement assistance as part of both Beginner Training and Professional Training programs.

Yes. Placement assistance is included along with training support such as live interactive sessions, real-time project experience, resume building support, mock interviews, hands-on projects, and interview preparation.

## What type of placement support is provided?

Tinitiate AI supports learners through resume building, mock interviews, interview preparation, real-time project experience, and practical hands-on training, which helps students become more job-ready.

## Does Tinitiate AI guarantee a job?

Tinitiate AI provides placement assistance, resume support, mock interviews, and interview preparation. Placement assistance means support and guidance for job readiness, but a final job offer depends on the student’s skills, interview performance, company requirements, and hiring process.

Yes. Mock interviews are included in both Beginner and Professional Training programs to help students practice and improve their interview confidence.

## Will Tinitiate AI help with resume preparation?

Yes. Resume building support is included as part of the training and placement assistance process.

## Does the training include interview preparation?

Yes. Both Beginner and Professional Training courses include hands-on projects and interview preparation.

## How do real-time projects help in placements?

Real-time project experience helps students understand practical implementation, build confidence, improve resumes, and explain project work during interviews. Tinitiate AI training includes real-time project experience as part of the learning process.

## Who can benefit from placement assistance?

Placement assistance is useful for students, freshers, beginners, career starters, and professionals who want to improve their job readiness through practical training, resume preparation, and interview practice.

## Will students get support for job interviews?

Yes. Students get support through interview preparation and mock interviews, helping them prepare for technical and job-related interview rounds.

Students should attend live sessions regularly, complete hands-on projects, prepare their resume properly, practice mock interviews, revise technical concepts, and actively apply for suitable job opportunities.
